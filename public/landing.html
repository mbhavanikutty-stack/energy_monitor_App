Overview

This application is a personalized energy-saving assistant designed for environmentally conscious and budget-aware households. It addresses the common challenge of high energy bills by providing users with actionable, data-driven recommendations to reduce their energy consumption. By analyzing user-specific data, the application offers tailored advice, making it easier for households to save money and reduce their carbon footprint.
